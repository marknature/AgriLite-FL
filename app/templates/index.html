About Us

Improving Agriculture, Improving Lives, Cultivating Crops To Make Farmers Increase Profit in Africa.

AgriLite FL is an intelligent, lightweight agricultural analytics platform designed for resource-constrained environments in Africa and other developing regions. The system integrates Federated Learning (FL), Computer Vision (CV), and Natural Language Processing (NLP) to support smallholder farmers with actionable insights while maintaining data privacy and minimizing computational costs. The platform focuses on enabling soil-based crop recommendations, crop disease diagnosis, and yield prediction, enhanced with a conversational AI chatbot that provides localized support in multiple languages.
